You may have seen Baccarat being played by James Bond himself and you too can feel like a secret agent when playing this classic casino game. The two parties involved are the player and the banker and goal is to get as close to 9 as possible. undefined

All cards have values and 10, jack, queen and king have the value of zero, while numbers 1 through 9 have their actual value. The ace has a value of 1. Two cards are drawn and whoever is the closest to 9 will win. undefined
